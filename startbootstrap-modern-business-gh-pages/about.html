How it all started!

Sam and Daniel are both passionate about cars and they live their lives by the quarter mile at a time. Both came out of highschool with the mindset of holding off on college for a year or two. They attended car rallies and car meetings more often with their free time. Eventually they came to the realization that they should put the money they have together and invest into a property. Soon the both found a beautiful location and in 2019, Sam and Daniel both opened a car dealership that is located in Bay Ridge, Brooklyn. The dealership has been a big success.
