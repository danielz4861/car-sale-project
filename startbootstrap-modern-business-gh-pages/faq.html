When should I get my car serviced?
We recommend owners to service their car every year or every 10,000-12,000 miles
When should I bring in my car for the New York State inspection?
Every car that is under New York plates have to come in once every year so we can inspect your car.
How long is the warranty?
The warranty covers 60,000 miles. After that, you can purchase warranty on your vehicle. We cover "bumper to bumper" meaning our warranty covers the whole car.
How do I check if the used vehicle has been into a car accident or have any problems?
Every vehicle has a VIN number and you can use the number to search for the vehicle's history using carfax.com
